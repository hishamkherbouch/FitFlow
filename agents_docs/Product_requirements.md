# Product Requirements
- **Primary Story:** As a gym-goer, I want one app to track my meals and lifts so an AI can explain my progress without me re-typing my data.
- **Must-Haves:**
  - Macro/Micro tracking with history.
  - Workout templates with "Last Time" weight/rep data.
  - AI Coach with read-access to logs.
- **Success:** User replaces MyFitnessPal and Hevy with this app.
